Top 25 Liberal Arts Colleges


Presented are the top 25 Liberal Arts colleges according to the most recent US News and World Report rankings. When you click on a blue bubble, it brings up the school's name, ranking, and a link to the corresponding wikipedia page.

Colored discs with numbers group results when they are geographically close together.
